Programs

From computer-based design to hands-on skills, we offer many different programs for all interests. We are continously updating our listings, so make sure to check back often or follow us on social media to learn about new programs!

Digital Imaging with Adobe

Unlock the power of digital imaging and unleash your creativity with our comprehensive course focused on Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign. Master essential image editing techniques, including cropping, retouching, and color correction while using easy to learn features such as layers, masks, and blending modes. This class is designed for individuals who want to explore the world of visual storytelling and enhance their digital imaging skills using Adobe's industry-standard software.


Typography 101

Embark on a typographic journey and unravel the intricate world of letterforms in our Typography 101 course. This class is tailored for individuals eager to master the principles and techniques that underpin effective typography across various design mediums. You will explore the anatomy of type, including serifs, sans serifs, and decorative elements, while developing an understanding of the psychology of fonts and their impact on communication. By the end of this course, students will be equipped with the foundational skills needed to wield the power of letters and transform ordinary text into visually compelling design elements.


Web Design Essentials

Ever wanted to make your own website? Embark on a dynamic exploration of the fundamentals of web design with Web Design Essentials. Familiarize yourself with tools including text editors, graphic software, and design prototyping tools, and build a solid foundation of the basics of HTML and CSS. Made for aspiring designers and developers, this class is designed to equip you with the important skills needed to create visually appealing, responsive, and user-friendly websites.


Printing Workshop

Step into the captivating world of printmaking with our immersive workshop designed for artists and enthusiasts eager to explore the diverse realm of print techniques. This is an engaging course that delves into both traditional and contemporary printing methods, offering participants a hands-on experience in creating unique, tactile artworks. Get to learn and understand the various types of printmaking, including relief, intaglio, lithography, and serigraphy. Whatever your level of artistry, this course will inspire you to push the boundaries of your creativity and leave you with a collection of unique and visually compelling prints.
