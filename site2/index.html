Welcome to After School Design, where creativity knows no bounds!

A creative after-school outlet for kids in low income neighborhoods.




The vibrant and dynamic space we create with students is dedicated to nurturing the artistic talents of our young artists. Guided by passionate instructors, students explore the world of graphic design, learning the fundamentals of layout, color theory, and digital tools. Whether designing eye-catching posters, crafting captivating social media graphics, or even bringing their unique illustrations to life, our programs provide a platform for students to express themselves visually.
