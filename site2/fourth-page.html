Our Team


Meet the Team Behind After School Design!

Dedicated to helping kids learn about everything graphic design has to offer.


Ethan Thompson
CEO

Under Ethan's visionary leadership, After School Design has flourished into a dynamic learning environment that goes beyond a design club. The program seamlessly integrates academic support with enriching extracurricular activities, fostering a holistic approach to education. Ethan's knowledge from his Master's degree in Visual Communication Design and commitment to creating a nurturing and inclusive space has made After School Design a beacon of opportunity for students of all backgrounds.

Harper Anderson
COO

Having a deep-rooted passion for design and an interest in the community is what started Harper's involvement in After School Design. With a Master's in Graphic Design and experience working with local philantropies, she brings to our program the belief that learning should extend beyond the classroom, providing a platform for students to explore their interests and talents in a supportive and nurturing environment. Her dedication to the Philadelphia community is what we strive for every day.

Jackson Williams
Operations Manager

Jackson's strategic vision extends beyond the classroom walls. A Master's student in Graphic Design and minor in business, he has forged partnerships with local businesses, community leaders, and educational experts to enhance the program's impact. Through his tireless efforts, After School Design has become a cornerstone of the community, providing not only academic support but also a sense of belonging and empowerment for the students it serves.

Mia Rodriguez
Education Specialist

With a background in design and a Master's degree in Educational Leadership, Mia brings a positive impact on the lives of young learners. Her journey began as a dedicated teacher, where she witnessed firsthand the challenges faced by students outside the traditional classroom setting. Motivated by a desire to bridge the gap between formal education and real-world skills, Mia joined After School Design to help adolescence learn what they're capable of.
